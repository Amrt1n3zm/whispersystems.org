Security made simple

A full replacement for your default text messaging application.

It's got everything you already do & love, and by default private & secure.

Focus on connecting

No new account, username, or password needed. Use your existing number, and contacts. If your contacts are available for a private conversation, the app will let you know.

Encrypted everything

All messages are encrypted locally, so if your phone is lost, your messages will be safe. Messages to other RedPhone users are encrypted over the air, protecting your communication in transit.

Whisper Labs

We are constantly experimenting with ways in which we can communicate privately & securely. Below is a glimpse of what we are currently brewing.

Zones

A mashup of geofencing and anti-theft security features, Zones will allow your phone react accordingly when crossing friendly or hostile borders that you define. For example, a phone inside a police station may wipe its call and message history, clear its contacts list, or even trigger a super-lock state, essentially disabling use until the device has returned to a trusted location.

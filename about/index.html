about us

Open Whisper Systems is two things: a large community of privacy advocates working to make mobile communications more secure, and a small team of dedicated developers, who maintain a best-in-class security protocol and related client software. How do you know it's safe? You can audit it yourself, or trust the experts.

Do we work for Twitter? No.
Does our software cost money to use? Nope.

Our mission is to make privacy simple and the best that it can be. It is also our mission to stay weird.

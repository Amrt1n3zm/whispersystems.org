Our deal

Open Whisper Systems is making private communication simple. We design open protocols, develop Open Source software, and give it away for free.

It's challenging work, and we pay ourselves to take it on, but we're not a business. We started this project because we think making things is fun, privacy is valuable, and cryptography is remarkable.

Working at Open Whisper Systems is an opportunity to collaborate with passionate people in an environment where it's possible to be creative and effective.

Highlights

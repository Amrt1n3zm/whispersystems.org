Focus on connecting

No new account, username, or password needed. Use your existing number, and contacts. If your contacts are available for a private conversation, the app will let you know.

Encrypted everything

All messages are encrypted locally, so if your phone is lost, your messages will be safe. Messages to other TextSecure users are encrypted over the air, protecting your communication in transit.

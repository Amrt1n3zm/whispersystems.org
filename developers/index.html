About our technology & how we work

Technical details

Our apps provide end-to-end encryption for mobile communications. Technical specifications can be found on Github for our calling and texting protocols. We are constantly working to improve the security of your data. To stay up to date on our latest advancements in mobile encryption, check out the blog.

Open Source Community

Open source means that anyone is welcome to contribute. Whether you are a designer, a developer, or simply eager to help, there are lots of ways to pitch in. Help reclaim privacy by bringing secure communication to everyone.

How to get involved.

Coins for code

Interested in contributing code? We pay Bitcoin! We’ve written and deployed a simple service called “BitHub” to pool and distribute Bitcoin donations to anyone who commits to our repositories.
